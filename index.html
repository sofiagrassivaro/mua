About me

Hi! I'm Sofia Grassivaro, a professional hair and makeup artist with a keen eye for color, shape, and pattern. My creative process is deeply influenced by art and design—I draw inspiration from a wide range of artists and art movements, embedding their visual language and conceptual messages into my work. I believe that looking beyond the traditional world of makeup is essential for pushing boundaries and cultivating true creativity.

I graduated from Arts University Bournemouth with a specialization in fashion, editorial, prosthetics, and hairstyling. My approach as a makeup artist ranges from clean, minimal looks—ideal for skincare and beauty brands—to bold, innovative styles designed for high fashion setting. I love collaborating with designers, brands, and photographers to bring unique concepts to life across both the fashion and film industries.


A key goal in my practice is to integrate prosthetics more prominently into fashion—especially on the runway—blurring the lines between beauty, character, and artistic expression.
